🛕 Jagannath Temple

Location: Puri, Odisha

Deity: Lord Jagannath

📜 History

The Jagannath Temple in Puri is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra, and is famous for the Rath Yatra festival...

🕉️ Rituals & Pooja

Daily rituals include Mangala Aarti, Abakash, Mailam, and Bhoga Mandap, with grand ceremonies during Rath Yatra...

⏰ Darshan Timings

Morning Darshan: 5:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Evening Darshan: 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM

⚖️ Visitor Guidelines

Non-Hindus are not permitted inside the sanctum. Devotees should follow temple dress codes and respect traditions...
