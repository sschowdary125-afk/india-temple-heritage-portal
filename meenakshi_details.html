🛕 Meenakshi Temple

Location: Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Deity: Goddess Meenakshi

📜 History

The Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar, renowned for its colorful gopurams...

🕉️ Rituals & Pooja

Daily rituals include Thiruvanandal, Kalasanthi, Uchikala Pooja, and Ardhajama Pooja, with vibrant celebrations during Navaratri...

⏰ Darshan Timings

Morning Darshan: 5:00 AM – 12:30 PM
Evening Darshan: 4:00 PM – 10:00 PM

⚖️ Visitor Guidelines

Photography is allowed in outer areas but restricted inside sanctum. Devotees should wear modest attire and maintain decorum...
