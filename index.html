Temple Listings

🛕 Tirupati Balaji

📍 Location: Andhra Pradesh

🙏 Deity: Lord Venkateswara

Known as one of the richest and most visited temples in India.

🛕 Kashi Vishwanath

📍 Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

🙏 Deity: Lord Shiva

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, revered as the spiritual heart of India.

🛕 Jagannath Temple

📍 Location: Puri, Odisha

🙏 Deity: Lord Jagannath

Famous for the grand Rath Yatra, symbolizing devotion and unity.

🛕 Meenakshi Temple

📍 Location: Madurai, Tamil Nadu

🙏 Deity: Goddess Meenakshi & Lord Sundareswarar

Renowned for its colorful gopurams and vibrant cultural festivals.

🕉️ Maha Shivratri

Maha Shivratri is dedicated to Lord Shiva, celebrated with night‑long prayers and temple rituals at Kashi Vishwanath.

🎉 Rath Yatra

Rath Yatra is the grand chariot festival of Lord Jagannath, celebrated in Puri with massive processions and devotional songs.

🌸 Navaratri

Navaratri is a nine‑night festival dedicated to Goddess Durga, celebrated with vibrant dances, rituals, and devotion at Meenakshi Temple.

🙏 Brahmotsavam

Brahmotsavam is the grand annual festival at Tirupati Balaji, featuring processions, rituals, and devotional celebrations that attract millions of pilgrims.
